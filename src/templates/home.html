About

BrickBots is an online eLearning platform focused on the technical skill development of architecture, engineering and construction professionals and with the purpose of advancing the knowledge base across the AEC Industry. Using high quality, personalized educational content, we seek to support professional development with continuing education courses in new and innovative topics, such as 3D Modeling, Rapid Prototyping, Visualization, Computational Design and Big Data/Cloud Management.

Our Mission

We are focused on delivering the highest quality educational content to compliment professional career development and continuing education with the ultimate goal of building better cities, towns and places to improve the everyday human experience.
